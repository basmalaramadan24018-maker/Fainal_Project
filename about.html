Our Story

Building Dreams Into Reality Since 1955

Arab Contractors was founded in 1955 with a vision to transform the construction industry in the Middle East and Africa. Over the decades, we have grown from a small local contractor to one of the largest and most respected construction companies in the region.

Our journey has been marked by landmark projects that have shaped cities and improved lives. From building critical infrastructure to creating iconic architectural landmarks, we take pride in every project we undertake.
